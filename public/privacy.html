Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-10-12

Game Over Gauge (“we”, “our”, “us”) provides a public dashboard that summarizes market indicators. We do not sell personal data. This page describes what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Anonymous analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate usage (page views, device types, referrers). GA may set cookies or use similar tech. IPs are truncated/aggregated by Google.
  • Server logs: Our hosting provider may log requests (e.g., IP address, user‑agent, timestamp) for reliability and abuse prevention. Logs rotate automatically.
  • No accounts, no forms: We do not run user accounts or collect form submissions.

How we use data

  • Operate and improve the site (performance, debugging, security).
  • Measure non‑personal, aggregate traffic (what pages are popular).

Third‑party services

  • Google Analytics – site analytics. See Google’s policies for details and controls.
  • Netlify – static site hosting and CDN.
  • GitHub Pages – alternate static hosting.

Cookies and consent

Analytics cookies may be used. You can block cookies in your browser settings. If you are located in regions that require consent (e.g., EEA), your browser or extensions can enforce your preferences.

Data retention

We keep logs only as long as necessary for operations and security. Aggregate analytics data may be retained by Google per its default retention settings.

Your choices

  • Use a browser with tracking protection or an ad‑blocker.
  • Opt‑out of Google Analytics with the official opt‑out add‑on.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/dkryuchkov/game-over-gauge.
